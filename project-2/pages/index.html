Potatoes O'Brien has been a family favorite and let me tell you my grandmother, Joan Curtin, perfected it! If you have ever had the Irish Potatoes O'Brien then you will know how much work and time goes into cutting and preparing the ingredients. Well not anymore! If you are anything like me I don't have the time to cut and prepare ingredients all the time. Below is a quick easy way to prepare Potatoes O'Briend with minimal work put into prepration, but the same delicious taste! Enjoy!

Ingredients needed



  1. 1 Package Frozen Potatoes O'Brien
  2. 1 Cup Sour Cream
  3. 1 Cup Cream Soup (Chicken, Celery or Mushroom)
  4. 1 Cup Grated Cheddar Cheese
  5. Green Onions

Simple Instructions
